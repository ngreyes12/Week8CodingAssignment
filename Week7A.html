The Demon Slayer Hashira

The Demon Slayer Corps is a group of people who hunt down demons at night to protect innocent civilians. Although they are not recognized by the government, they are led by Kagaya Ubuyashiki and undergo covert operations. The Hashira are conseidered the 9 pillars of the Demon Slayers because they are the strongest. Their powers are rivaled by the 12 Kizuki, which are the strongest demons alive.
